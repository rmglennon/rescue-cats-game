Rescue the Cats Game

Collect the cats, but they will escape if you try to collect too many at once!

Game rules

You will be given a random number of cats to rescue at the start of the game.

There are four cats below. By clicking on a cat, you will rescue a specific amount of cats and add points to your score. You win the game by exactly matching your cats collected to the random number you were directed to rescue. You lose if you rescue too many cats and your score is higher than the random number.

The value of each cat click is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the values of the cats will change.
