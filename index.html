Rescue the Cats Game

Collect the cats, but don't try to take too many at once.

Game rules

You will be given a random number of cats to collect at the start of the game.

There are four cats below. By clicking on a cat, you will collect a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total number of cats collected to the random number you were supposed to collect. You lose the game if you collect more cats that your house can hold and your total score is higher than the random number.

The value of each cat is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each cat.
